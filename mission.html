Our Mission

Our mission is to promote transparency and accountability in academic research by making biological sex representation measurable.

Scientific research shapes healthcare, public policy, education, and technological advancement. Ensuring balanced representation strengthens the reliability and fairness of research outcomes.

This tool encourages awareness of representation gaps and supports efforts toward more inclusive and equitable scientific practices.

By providing accessible analytical tools, we aim to empower students, researchers, and institutions to critically evaluate representation in published work.
