About

The Female Representation Analysis Tool was created to evaluate biological sex representation within academic research papers.

Research findings influence medical treatments, policies, funding priorities, and scientific understanding. However, many studies historically underrepresent female participants, leading to gaps in knowledge and biased conclusions.

This tool provides a transparent, standardized way to analyze participant representation using publicly available research documents.

By making representation measurable and visible, we aim to promote awareness, accountability, and equity in scientific research.
